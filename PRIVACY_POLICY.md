## 🔒 Overview

THE V ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

## 👤 Your Rights

### Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access Your Data**
   - Request all data we store about you
   - Available within 30 days

2. **Correct Your Data**
   - Update inaccurate information
   - Edit your profile anytime

3. **Delete Your Data**
   - Request account deletion
   - We'll delete account data (may retain backups for limited time)
   - Some data may be retained for legal compliance

4. **Export Your Data**
   - Download your posts, messages, and profile
   - In portable format
   - Available within 30 days

5. **Restrict Processing**
   - Opt-out of marketing communications
   - Disable location tracking
   - Manage notification preferences

6. **Withdraw Consent**
   - For optional data collection and processing
   - Available anytime in Settings

### How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact us at: [support@thev.com](mailto:support@thev.com) with:
- Your request (access, correction, deletion, export)
- Account username
- Verification of identity

## 🗑️ Data Retention

### We Keep Your Data For:

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|-----------------|--------|
| Account Information | Duration of account + 30 days | Legal compliance, recovery option |
| Posts & Stories | Duration of account + 90 days | User recovery, archive |
| Messages | Duration of account + 30 days | User recovery |
| Deleted Content | 30 days | Recovery before permanent deletion |
| Analytics | 12 months | Legal compliance, fraud detection |
| Backups | 30 days | Disaster recovery |
| Error Logs | 7 days | Bug fixes |

### Deletion Process
1. You request account deletion
2. We anonymize personal data
3. Posts can be downloaded before deletion
4. Account marked as deleted (30 day grace period)
5. Permanent deletion after 30 days
6. Some data retained for legal compliance
